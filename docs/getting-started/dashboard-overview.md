## 4. Conversation History Panel

Access your past conversations.

[Screenshot: Conversation history panel]

### How It Works

**View your history:**
1. Click "History" or "Conversations" in the sidebar
2. Your past chats appear in chronological order
3. Click any conversation to reopen it

**What you'll see for each conversation:**
- ğŸ“Œ **Title** - Auto-generated from first message
- ğŸ• **Date/Time** - When you started the chat
- ğŸ’¬ **Preview** - First few words of the conversation

**Actions you can take:**
- **Open** - Continue the conversation
- **Rename** - Give it a custom title
- **Delete** - Remove permanently
- **Export** - Save as PDF or text file

[Screenshot: Conversation history item with action menu]

### Search Your History

**Find old conversations:**
1. Look for the **search box** at the top of History panel
2. Type keywords from the conversation
3. Results appear instantly

Example: Search "invoice" to find all chats about invoices

## Interface Customization

### Theme Options

**Choose your preferred appearance:**

**Light Mode** (Default)
- Bright, clean interface
- Better for well-lit environments
- Easier on the eyes during day

**Dark Mode**
- Dark background, light text
- Reduces eye strain in low light
- Popular for extended use

**How to change theme:**
1. Click your profile icon (top-right)
2. Select "Settings" or "Preferences"
3. Choose "Appearance" or "Theme"
4. Select Light or Dark
5. Changes apply immediately

[Screenshot: Theme selection in settings]
