## System Requirements

### Supported Web Browsers

Neurond Assistant works best with modern web browsers. We recommend:

| Browser | Minimum Version |
|---------|----------------|
| **Google Chrome** | Version 90+ (Recommended) |
| **Microsoft Edge** | Version 90+ |
| **Mozilla Firefox** | Version 88+ |
| **Safari** | Version 14+ |

:::tip Best Experience
For the best performance, use the **latest version** of Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
:::

### Device Requirements

- **Desktop/Laptop:** Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, or Linux (any recent distribution)
- **Tablet:** iPad (iOS 14+), Android tablets (Android 10+)
- **Mobile:** While accessible on phones, we recommend using a tablet or computer for the best experience

### Internet Connection

- Stable internet connection (or internal network access)
- Minimum speed: 5 Mbps (10+ Mbps recommended for document upload)

## Finding Neurond Assistant

### Step 1: Get Your Access URL

Your organization's Neurond Assistant is accessed through a unique web address (URL). This will be provided by your IT department and typically looks like:

**Examples:**
- `https://assistant.yourcompany.com`
- `https://neurond.yourcompany.com`
- `https://yourcompany-na.vercel.app`
- `https://ai.yourcompany.internal` (for internal networks)

:::caution Important
**Save this URL** to your browser's bookmarks for easy access. Each organization has its own unique address.
:::

[Screenshot: Browser address bar showing Neurond Assistant URL]

### Step 2: Open the URL in Your Browser

1. Open your preferred web browser
2. Type or paste the Neurond Assistant URL in the address bar
3. Press **Enter**

You should now see the Neurond Assistant login page.

## Authentication Methods

Your organization uses one of the following login methods:

### Method 1: Single Sign-On (SSO)

**Most Common Method**

If your organization uses SSO, you'll log in with your existing company credentials.

**Steps:**
1. Click "Login with SSO" or "Sign in with [Company Name]"
2. You'll be redirected to your company's login page
3. Enter your company username and password
4. You may need to complete two-factor authentication (2FA) if required
5. You'll be automatically redirected back to Neurond Assistant

[Screenshot: SSO login button and redirect flow]

**Advantage:** One set of credentials for all company systems!

---

### Method 2: Username and Password

**Direct Login**

If your organization uses direct authentication:

**Steps:**
1. Enter the **username** provided by your administrator
2. Enter your **password**
3. Click "Sign In"

**On first login**, you may be required to:
- Change your temporary password
- Set up security questions
- Configure your profile

[Screenshot: Username and password login form]

---

### Method 3: Email Magic Link

**Passwordless Option**

Some organizations use email-based authentication:

**Steps:**
1. Enter your **company email address**
2. Click "Send Magic Link"
3. Check your email inbox
4. Click the secure link in the email
5. You'll be logged in automatically

:::note Email Delivery
Magic link emails usually arrive within 1-2 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
:::

[Screenshot: Magic link email interface]

## Troubleshooting Access Issues

### Cannot Find the Login Page

**Problem:** The URL doesn't load or shows an error

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Double-check the URL spelling with your IT department
- ‚úì Make sure you're on the company network (VPN may be required if working remotely)
- ‚úì Try a different browser
- ‚úì Clear your browser cache and cookies
- ‚úì Check if your company firewall is blocking access

---

### Login Credentials Don't Work

**Problem:** Username/password rejected

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Verify your username (case-sensitive)
- ‚úì Check Caps Lock is **off** when typing password
- ‚úì Use "Forgot Password" link if available
- ‚úì Contact your system administrator to reset credentials
- ‚úì Confirm your account has been activated

---

### SSO Redirect Fails

**Problem:** SSO login redirects but doesn't complete

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Allow pop-ups in your browser for the SSO domain
- ‚úì Clear cookies for the SSO provider
- ‚úì Try in an incognito/private browser window
- ‚úì Verify you're using a supported browser version
- ‚úì Contact IT if the SSO provider appears to be down

---

### Page Loads Slowly or Freezes

**Problem:** Login page is slow or unresponsive

**Solutions:**
- ‚úì Check your internet connection speed
- ‚úì Close unnecessary browser tabs
- ‚úì Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- ‚úì Restart your browser
- ‚úì Try a different browser

## Remote Access and VPN

### Working from Home

If you're working remotely, you may need to:

1. **Connect to VPN first** (if required by your organization)
   - Launch your company's VPN client
   - Connect to the corporate network
   - Then access Neurond Assistant

2. **Check with IT** if you have access issues
   - Some organizations restrict access to office networks only
   - Others allow full remote access
   - VPN configuration may be required

:::important Security Note
Never share your login credentials with others, even colleagues. Each person should have their own account.
:::
