This guide walks you through the complete login process for Neurond Assistant, from your first login to understanding your user dashboard.

## Purpose

**This guide helps you:**
- Complete your first successful login
- Understand the login process
- Set up your user profile (if applicable)
- Know what to expect after logging in

Make sure you have:
- ‚úì Accessed the Neurond Assistant URL (see [Accessing Neurond](accessing-neurond.md))
- ‚úì Your login credentials ready (username/password or SSO access)

## Login Methods

Your organization determines which login method is used. Follow the instructions for your method:

### Method A: Single Sign-On (SSO) Login

**Most organizations use this method**

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

**1. Navigate to Neurond Assistant**
- Open your browser and go to your organization's Neurond Assistant URL

[Screenshot: Neurond Assistant login page with SSO button]

**2. Click the SSO Login Button**
- Look for a button that says:
  - "Login with SSO"
  - "Sign in with [Your Company Name]"
  - "Company Login"
  - "Single Sign-On"

[Screenshot: SSO login button highlighted]

**3. Complete Company Authentication**
- You'll be redirected to your company's login page
- Enter your **company username** (same as email or other company systems)
- Enter your **company password**
- Click "Sign In" or "Login"

[Screenshot: Company SSO authentication page]

**4. Two-Factor Authentication (if required)**
- If your organization uses 2FA, you'll see an additional verification step
- Enter the code from:
  - Your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator)
  - SMS text message
  - Email
- Click "Verify" or "Submit"

[Screenshot: 2FA code entry screen]

**5. Grant Permissions (first-time only)**
- You may see a permission request screen
- This allows Neurond Assistant to access your basic profile info
- Click "Allow" or "Authorize"

[Screenshot: Permission authorization screen]

**6. Automatic Redirect**
- You'll be automatically redirected back to Neurond Assistant
- Wait a moment while the system loads

---

### Method B: Username and Password Login

**Direct authentication method**

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

**1. Navigate to Login Page**
- Go to your Neurond Assistant URL
- You'll see a login form with username and password fields

[Screenshot: Login form with username and password fields]

**2. Enter Your Username**
- Type your username exactly as provided by your administrator
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Usernames are **case-sensitive**
- Do not include spaces before or after

[Screenshot: Username field filled in]

**3. Enter Your Password**
- Type your password carefully
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Passwords are **case-sensitive**
- Check that Caps Lock is OFF
- Password characters will appear as dots (‚Ä¢‚Ä¢‚Ä¢) for security

[Screenshot: Password field with masked characters]

**4. Click "Sign In"**
- Click the "Sign In", "Login", or "Submit" button
- Wait a moment while the system verifies your credentials

[Screenshot: Sign In button highlighted]

**5. First-Time Password Change (if required)**
- If you're using a temporary password, you'll be prompted to create a new one
- Follow the password requirements shown on screen (typically):
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - At least one uppercase letter
  - At least one lowercase letter
  - At least one number
  - At least one special character (!, @, #, etc.)

[Screenshot: Password change screen with requirements]

---

### Method C: Email Magic Link

**Passwordless authentication**

#### Step-by-Step Instructions

**1. Navigate to Login Page**
- Go to your Neurond Assistant URL
- Look for "Login with Email" or "Send Magic Link" option

[Screenshot: Magic link login option]

**2. Enter Your Email Address**
- Type your **company email address**
- This must be the email registered with Neurond Assistant
- Click "Send Magic Link"

[Screenshot: Email input field for magic link]

**3. Check Your Email**
- Open your email inbox
- Look for an email from Neurond Assistant (usually arrives within 1-2 minutes)
- Subject line might be: "Your Neurond Assistant Login Link"

[Screenshot: Email inbox showing magic link email]

**4. Click the Secure Link**
- Open the email
- Click the "Sign In" or "Access Neurond Assistant" button/link
- The link typically expires in 10-15 minutes

[Screenshot: Magic link email content with button]

**5. Automatic Login**
- You'll be automatically logged in
- No password required!

:::caution Link Expiration
Magic links expire quickly for security. If your link expires, simply request a new one by repeating the process.
:::

## After Successful Login

### What You'll See

Once logged in successfully, you'll be directed to the **Neurond Assistant Dashboard**.

**Main Elements:**
1. **Welcome Message** with your name
2. **Chat Interface** in the center
3. **Navigation Menu** on the left (or top)
4. **User Profile** icon (usually top-right corner)
5. **Conversation History** (sidebar or menu)

[Screenshot: Neurond Assistant dashboard after first login]

### Expected Result

‚úÖ **Success looks like:**
- You see your name or profile picture
- The chat interface is ready ("Type your message..." placeholder)
- Navigation menus are visible
- No error messages displayed

‚ùå **If login fails:**
- Error message appears (see Troubleshooting below)

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

### "Invalid Credentials" Error

**Problem:** Username or password not recognized

**Quick Fixes:**
1. ‚úì Check for typos in username
2. ‚úì Verify Caps Lock is OFF
3. ‚úì Try copying and pasting to avoid spaces
4. ‚úì Use "Forgot Password" link
5. ‚úì Contact your administrator if still failing

---

### "Account Not Found" Error

**Problem:** System doesn't recognize your account

**Quick Fixes:**
1. ‚úì Verify you've been granted access (contact admin)
2. ‚úì Check if you're using the correct email domain
3. ‚úì Ensure your account has been activated
4. ‚úì Wait 24 hours if access was just granted (sync time)

---

### SSO Redirect Loop

**Problem:** Keeps redirecting back and forth

**Quick Fixes:**
1. ‚úì Clear browser cookies and cache
2. ‚úì Allow pop-ups from the SSO domain
3. ‚úì Try in incognito/private mode
4. ‚úì Check if SSO provider is experiencing issues
5. ‚úì Contact IT support

---

### Magic Link Not Received

**Problem:** Email with login link doesn't arrive

**Quick Fixes:**
1. ‚úì Wait 2-3 minutes (may be delayed)
2. ‚úì Check spam/junk folder
3. ‚úì Verify you entered the correct email
4. ‚úì Request a new link
5. ‚úì Contact IT if still not received

---

### "Session Expired" Message

**Problem:** Logged out unexpectedly

**Quick Fixes:**
1. ‚úì Simply log in again
2. ‚úì This happens after periods of inactivity (security feature)
3. ‚úì Your work is automatically saved
4. ‚úì Consider extending session timeout (ask admin)

---

## Session and Security

### Session Duration

**How long you stay logged in:**
- **Typical session:** 8-12 hours of activity
- **Inactivity timeout:** Usually 30-60 minutes
- **"Remember Me" option:** May keep you logged in for days (if available)

### Security Best Practices

:::tip Stay Secure
1. **Never share** your login credentials
2. **Log out** when using shared computers
3. **Lock your screen** when stepping away
4. **Report suspicious activity** to IT immediately
5. **Change password regularly** (if using password method)
:::
