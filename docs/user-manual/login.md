This guide walks you through the process of opening and logging into Neurond Assistant.

## 1. How to Open Neurond Assistant

Opening Neurond Assistant is as easy as opening your favorite website. You don't need to install any special software!

### What you need
- **A Computer or Tablet:** Any computer that you use for work will do.
- **Internet:** You need to be connected to the internet.
- **A Web Browser:** We recommend using **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** for the best experience.

### Where is the link?
Navigate to the URL provided by your IT department (e.g., `https://assistant.yourcompany.com`).

**Tip:** Click the star icon (⭐️) in your browser's address bar to bookmark the page for next time.

### Troubleshooting Access
If you see a blank screen or error:
- **Refresh the page:** Click the reload button (⟳).
- **Check your internet:** Try opening another website.

### Login Methods

Depending on your organization's configuration, you can log in using one of these methods:

#### Option A: Single Sign-On (SSO) - *Recommended*
- **How it works:** Click "Login with Company Account" (or "Login with Microsoft"/"Google").
- **What to do:** Use the same email and password you use for your other company apps.

#### Option B: Email and Password
- **How it works:** You type in your company email and a special password for this app.
- **What to do:** Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**, then click **Sign In**.

### First-time Setup
The first time you log in, you might be asked to:
- **Agree to the rules:** Click "I Agree" to acceptable use policies.
- **Set up your profile:** Confirm your name so the assistant knows how to address you.

---

## 3. Help! I can't get in

Don't worry, even the smartest people get locked out sometimes!
- **Did you misspell your email?** Check for typos.
- **Is Caps Lock on?** Passwords are case-sensitive.
- **Forgot password?** Use the "Forgot Password?" link to reset it via email.
- **Still stuck?** Contact your IT support team.

---

## 4. Security Considerations
*   Do not share your password with anyone.
*   Log out after completing your session if using a shared computer.

---

## 5. How to Log Out Safely

When you are finished using Neurond Assistant, it is important to log out to keep your information safe.

1.  **Locate your profile:** Click on your account logo at the **bottom left** corner of the sidebar.
2.  **Sign Out:** In the menu that appears, click the **Sign Out** button (marked with an arrow icon).
