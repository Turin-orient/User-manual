The Prompt Gallery is a powerful tool for managing and discovering AI prompts. It allows you to save your own creative prompts or use community-wide "Global" prompts to enhance your interaction with Neurond Assistant.

## 1. Overview of the Gallery

When you access the Prompt Gallery, you are presented with a library of prompts. You can switch between viewing **Global** prompts (created by administrators) and **My Prompts** (your personal collection).

![Default Prompt Gallery UI](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Default_prompt_gallery_ui.png)

### Global vs. Private Modes
*   **Global Mode**: High-quality, verified prompts available to everyone in your organization.
    ![Global Prompts](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Global_prompt_mode.png)
*   **My Prompts**: A private space for your own custom prompts.
    ![My Prompts Mode](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Default_user's_prompt_mode.png)

## 2. Creating Your Own Prompts

To create a new prompt, click the **+ Add** button at the top of the interface.

<AnnotatedImage 
  src="/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Click_add_to_create_prompt.png" 
  alt="Add New Prompt Button"

### The Creation Process
1.  **Name & Content**: Give your prompt a descriptive name and enter the detailed instructions for the AI.
2.  **Add Tags**: Organize your prompts using tags. Click the add button in the tags section to create or select categories.
    ![Add Tag Detail](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/click_add_to_add_tag.png)
3.  **Save**: Once finished, your prompt will appear in your private gallery.

![Sample of Creating a Prompt](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Sample_of_creating_prompt.png)
![Gallery After Creation](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/My_prompt_mode_after_create_prompt.png)

## 3. Efficiently Using Prompts

### Searching and Filtering
Use the search bar at the top to quickly find prompts by name or content.

![Search Prompts](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Can_search_prompt_on_search_bar.png)

### Viewing and Copying
Click on a prompt card to expand it. This allows you to view the full prompt content and quickly copy it to your clipboard for use in the chat interface.

![Expand and View Prompt](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Click_to_expand_prompt_to_view_and_copy.png)

## 4. Managing Your Collection

### Selecting Prompts
Click the **Select** button to enter management mode.

![Entering Select Mode](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/When_user_clicks_Select_button.png)

### Batch Deleting
You can select one or more prompts that you created to delete them from your gallery.

![Selecting Prompts to Delete](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/Select_one_or_more_prompts_to_delete.png)

Upon successful deletion, you will receive a confirmation notification.

![Deletion Successful Notification](/img/ui/prompt-gallery/deleted_prompt_successfull_status.png)
