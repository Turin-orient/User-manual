# What is Neurond Assistant?

Welcome to the Neurond Assistant User Manual. This guide will help you understand and use Neurond Assistant effectively in your daily work.

## Overview

**Neurond Assistant (NA)** is your company's intelligent digital employee. Unlike public AI tools like ChatGPT or Google Gemini, Neurond Assistant is specifically designed and trained to understand your company's:
- Internal documents and policies
- Business processes and workflows
- Industry-specific terminology
- Company standards and guidelines

Think of Neurond Assistant as a knowledgeable colleague who has read every document in your company and is available 24/7 to help you work more efficiently.

:::tip Your Digital Employee
Neurond Assistant is like having an expert assistant who:
- **Never forgets** company policies or procedures
- **Works instantly** - no waiting for email responses
- **Stays consistent** - provides the same accurate information to everyone
- **Keeps learning** from your company's knowledge base
:::

## What Problems Does Neurond Assistant Solve?

### 1. **Finding Information Takes Too Long**
**Before:** Searching through folders, emails, and SharePoint sites for hours to find a specific policy or procedure.

**With Neurond Assistant:** Simply ask "What is our expense reimbursement policy?" and get an instant answer with references to the official documents.

### 2. **Repetitive Manual Tasks**
**Before:** Manually typing data from invoices into Excel spreadsheets, risking errors and wasting time.

**With Neurond Assistant:** Upload invoice images, and NA automatically extracts amounts, dates, and vendor information into a structured format.

### 3. **Inconsistent Information**
**Before:** Different employees giving different answers to the same question because they remember policies differently.

**With Neurond Assistant:** Everyone gets the same accurate answer based on your company's official documentation.

### 4. **Document Creation From Scratch**
**Before:** Spending time writing routine emails or reports, unsure about the right tone or format.

**With Neurond Assistant:** Request "Write a professional email to customer ABC about delivery delay" and get a well-written draft in seconds.
