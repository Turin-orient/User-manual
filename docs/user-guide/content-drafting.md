## Step-by-Step: Drafting an Email

**1. Describe What You Need**

```
"Write a professional email to the facilities team asking  
 them to fix the broken air conditioning in Conference Room B.  
 Mention it was reported yesterday and we have a client  
 meeting tomorrow."
```

**2. Review NA's Draft**

NA provides a complete email draft:

```
Subject: Urgent: AC Repair Needed in Conference Room B

Dear Facilities Team,

I'm writing to follow up on the air conditioning issue  
in Conference Room B, which was reported yesterday.

The AC unit is currently not functioning, and we have an  
important client meeting scheduled in that room tomorrow  
at 9 AM.Could you please prioritize this repair?

If the room cannot be fixed in time, please let me know  
so we can arrange an alternate location.

Thank you for your prompt attention.

Best regards,
[Your name]
```

**3. Request Revisions if Needed**

```
You: "Make it less formal"
You: "Add my phone number at the bottom"
You: "Make it shorter"
```

**4. Copy and Use**
- Copy the final draft
- Paste into your email client
- Send!

## Common Drafting Examples

### Example 1: Status Update Email

**Request:**
```
"Draft an email to my manager updating her on Project Phoenix.  
 Include:  
 - Completed: Requirements gathering, design mockups  
 - In progress: Development (50% done)  
 - Next: Testing phase starts Monday  
 - No blockers"
```

### Example 2: Meeting Agenda

**Request:**
```
"Create an agenda for next Tuesday's quarterly review meeting.  
 Topics: Sales performance, HR updates, Q1 budget review,  
 New product launch timeline. Meeting is 2 hours."
```

### Example 3: Client Apology

**Request:**
```
"Write an apologetic email to client ABC Corp explaining  
 their order will be 2 weeks late due to supply chain issues.  
 Offer a 10% discount . Professional and sincere tone."
```

### Example 4: Report Summary

**Request:**
```
"Summarize this 20-page market research report into a  
 1-page executive summary highlighting:  
 - Key findings  
 - Market opportunities  
 - Recommended actions"
```
