## Step-by-Step: Searching for Information

**1. Think of Your Question**
- What information do you need?
- From which topic area?

**2. Ask Neurond Assistant**
- Type your question in plain language
- Be specific about what you're looking for

**3. Review the Answer**
- NA searches relevant documents
- Provides answer with source citations
- Includes document names and sections

## Example Questions

### Company Policies
```
"What is our remote work policy?"
"What are the guidelines for expense reimbursement?"
"Explain our data security policy"
```

### Procedures
```
"How do I request time off?"
"What is the process for submitting an IT ticket?"
"How do I order office supplies?"
```

### People and Contacts
```
"Who is the HR manager?"
```

## When Information Isn't Found

**NA says:** "I don't have information about that in my knowledge base."

**Possible reasons:**
1. Document wasn't added to the knowledge base
2. Using different terminology than in documents
3. Information is in a system NA doesn't have access to
4. Topic is outside loaded content

**What to do:**
- âœ“ Rephrase your question
- âœ“ Try related keywords
- âœ“ Ask your supervisor or department
