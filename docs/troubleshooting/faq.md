# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to commonly asked questions about Neurond Assistant.

## General Questions

### What is Neurond Assistant?
Neurond Assistant is an AI-powered digital assistant customized for your organization. It helps you find information, process documents, and draft content using your company's specific knowledge base.

### How is it different from ChatGPT or Gemini?
- **Your data stays private** - runs on company infrastructure
- **Knows your company** - trained on internal documents
- **More accurate for your business** - understands company-specific processes
- **Controlled access** - managed by your IT team

### Is my data safe and private?
Yes! All conversations and documents stay within your company's systems. Data is never sent to public AI services or used to train public models.

### Can I use it on my phone?
Yes, Neurond Assistant works on mobile browsers, but the experience is optimized for desktop/laptop or tablet use.

## Using Neurond Assistant

### What can I ask Neurond Assistant?
- Company policies and procedures
- Help processing documents (invoices, forms, resumes)
- Drafting emails and reports
- Summarizing long documents
- Finding information in company knowledge base

### What languages does it support?
Typically English and Vietnamese. Check with your admin for specific language support.

### How do I ask better questions?
Be specific! Instead of "tell me about policies," ask "What is our remote work policy for full-time employees?"

### Can it access my email or other systems?
Only if specifically configured by your organization. By default, it works with uploaded documents and the company knowledge base.

### Where does the information come from?
From your company's knowledge base - documents uploaded by administrators, such as employee handbooks, policies, procedures, and company documentation.

## Documents and Files

### What file types can I upload?
- PDF, Word (.doc, .docx)
- Excel (.xls, .xlsx)
- PowerPoint (.ppt, .pptx)
- Images (.jpg, .png) for OCR
- Text files (.txt)

### What's the file size limit?
Usually 10-25 MB per file. Check with your admin for exact limits.

### Can NA read scanned PDFs?
Yes, if they use OCR (Optical Character Recognition). Plain image scans may be limited.

### What happens to my uploaded files?
They're processed for the current session and stored according to your company's data retention policy.

## Account and Access

### How do I get an account?
Contact your system administrator or IT help desk to request access.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or contact your admin for a reset.

### Why was I logged out?
Sessions expire after inactivity (usually 30-60 minutes) for security. Your work is automatically saved.

### Can I use Neurond Assistant from home?
Depends on your organization's policy. You may need VPN access. Check with IT.

## Features and Functionality

### Can NA remember previous conversations?
Yes! Your conversation history is saved and you can return to any previous chat.

### Can I export NA's responses?
Yes, you can export to PDF, Word, Excel, or copy/paste.

### Does NA make mistakes?
AI can occasionally provide incorrect information. Always review important outputs before using them.

### Can I give feedback on responses?
Yes, use the thumbs up/down buttons to rate responses and help improve the system.

## Troubleshooting

### NA says "I don't have information about that"
The topic may not be in the knowledge base. Try rephrasing or ask a related question. Contact admin if important documents are missing.

### My upload failed
Check file size and type. Ensure it's under the limit and in a supported format.

### Responses are very slow
Could be high system load. Try during off-peak hours, or contact admin if consistently slow.

### I'm getting error messages
Check [Error Messages Guide](error-messages.md) or [Common Issues](common-issues.md) for solutions.

## Security and Privacy

### Who can see my conversations?
Only you, unless you share them. Admins may have oversight for compliance purposes.

### Is communication encrypted?
Yes, all traffic uses HTTPS encryption.

### Where is data stored?
Data is stored on your organization's chosen infrastructure (Vercel, or self-hosted servers).

### What happens to deleted conversations?
They're removed according to your organization's data retention policy. Deletion may be permanent or archived.
