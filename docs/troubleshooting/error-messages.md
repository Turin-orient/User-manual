# Understanding Error Messages

Sometimes the screen might show a message that sounds a bit strange or scary. Don't worry! These messages are just the computer's way of telling you it needs a little help. Here is how to understand them in plain English.

---

## 1. Login Errors

### "Email or password incorrect"
- **What it means:** There's a typo in the email or password you typed.
- **What to do:** Double-check your spelling and make sure **Caps Lock** is turned off.

### "Account not found"
- **What it means:** The email you typed isn't registered in the system yet.
- **What to do:** Check if you're using your official company email. If so, ask your manager or IT to invite you to the app.

### "Session expired"
- **What it means:** You've been logged in for a long time without using the app, so it logged you out for safety.
- **What to do:** Just log in again. Everything is fine!

---

## 2. Connection Errors

### "Failed to connect to service"
- **What it means:** Your computer is having trouble "talking" to the assistant's brain.
- **What to do:** Wait 1 minute and refresh the page (click the circle arrow ‚ü≥ at the top).

### "Usage limit reached" (Quota exceeded)
- **What it means:** Our company has asked too many questions this month and we need to wait for a reset.
- **What to do:** Tell your manager or wait until next month.

### "You are asking too fast" (Rate limit)
- **What it means:** You're sending questions faster than the assistant can keep up with.
- **What to do:** Take a quick break to stretch or sip some coffee, then try again in 1-2 minutes.

---

## 3. File & Document Errors

### "File too large"
- **What it means:** The document you're trying to send is too heavy for the assistant to carry.
- **What to do:** Use a smaller file or split one big file into two smaller ones.

### "Unsupported file type"
- **What it means:** The assistant doesn't know how to read this type of file.
- **What to do:** Try converting your file to a **PDF** or a **Word** document.

---

## 4. Response Errors

### "I don't have information about that"
- **What it means:** The answer isn't in any of the company's documents.
- **What to do:** Try asking in a different way or use simpler words.

### "Error generating response"
- **What it means:** The assistant's "brain" had a small hiccup while thinking.
- **What to do:** Click the **"Try Again"** button (the little circle arrow icon) next to the message.

---

**Tip:** 90% of all problems go away if you simply **refresh the page** or **log back in!**
